Portfolio

Web Developer Brand Portfolio Website and Business Cards

For this website, I teamed up with a web developer colleague of mine in need of rebranding. He was liked fractals and patterns that were futuristic and technical, which inspired me to choose the stock photo shown on the splash page and use a different edited rendition for each succeeding content area. This simple one page website utilizes Bootstrap styling for navigation and responsiveness and normalize.css for a consistent brand accross browsers. In designing this website I learned how to use git as an organized way to manage my progress.

I decided to integrate what my colleague does into the look of the business card with a little attitude. The same stock photo used for the splash page of the website fills the back with UV coating. The front is an illustration of a JSON object, colors pulled from the image. My main challenge was ensuring all letters were legible swimming in a sea of black on such a small canvas-a risk I wanted to take and a decision I do not take lightly.

About Me

Hello, I'm Kelly. Thank you for checking out my online portfolio.

I could draw since I could hold a crayon. Watching Nick at Night before bed as a kid, I dreamed of doing what Darren from Bewitched did as a career. Remember him? He was an advertising illustrator. He would draw up these cheesy commercials on large boards, show them to his boss and his boss would say how lousy they were. Darren's boss's reaction didn't bother me-I knew I wanted to do that!

I hope to one day change the world through my professional, yet quirky advertising. In the meantime, however, I am available for a full time opportunity at a company that needs a team player with an eye for detail and keen focus on the client's needs.
